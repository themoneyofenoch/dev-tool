# FidelType App Icon — Design Brief

## Output
- Master 1024×1024 PNG + standard iOS sizes + Android adaptive icons

## Design
- **Background**: Solid #1A1A2E (dark navy)
- **Main element**: Large circle #D4A843 (gold), ~655×655 px, centered
- **Glyph inside circle**: Ge'ez letter "ፊ" (FI — first letter of ፊደል = alphabet), pure white, NotoSansEthiopic Bold, ~490 px, centered in the gold circle
- **Label below circle**: "FidelType" in gold #D4A843, Inter SemiBold or SF Pro, ~67 px, letter-spaced, centered near bottom

## Android Adaptive
- Background layer: solid #1A1A2E
- Foreground layer: gold circle + ፊ only (transparent bg), keep within center 72×72 dp safe zone

## Vibe
Premium keyboard app — dark + gold luxury, culturally authentic (Ge'ez letter), clean minimal.

## Deliverables
1. Master 1024×1024 PNG
2. iOS: 180, 152, 167, 120, 76, 60, 40, 29, 20 (all @2x/@3x variants)
3. Android: xxxhdpi(192) through mdpi(48) — foreground + background layers
